- Web developer -

My name is Maurizio Abatantuono, and I was born in Caracas, Venezuela. From an early age, I was involved with the world of computing, although it wasn't something I initially considered as a future career.

After finishing high school, I pursued my university studies in international business administration at the Interamerican University of Panama. However, upon graduating, I realized it wasn't for me. Despite being skilled with numbers, I wasn't content with what I was doing. That's when I discovered my true passion.

On one of those days when you question yourself about your strengths, flashes of my childhood memories related to computing came to mind. I remembered that at the age of 12, I had already created my first game servers and achieved remarkable things in the world of computing, all self-taught and as just a child. Repairing friends' computers and effortlessly configuring systems always came naturally to me; it was as if the machines and I spoke the same language.

At 29 years old, fueled by the same enthusiasm I had at 12, I wanted to understand how it was possible to create everything we see today in the digital world. That's when I fell in love with programming. I took courses in web development, JavaScript, backend, and ReactJS, in addition to countless hours studying autonomously on my computer without pause.

Today, I have had the opportunity to work on many projects. Although I am more focused on the frontend area, I have the ability to intervene and perform tasks in any programming field. What I don't know, I research and apply in a straightforward manner.

In summary, my journey has led me to discover my true passion for programming. I combine my self-taught experience with technical skills to tackle challenging projects. I am excited to continue learning and growing in this dynamic and rewarding field.
